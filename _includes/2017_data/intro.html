Welcoming Remarks

Building upon the successes of last year's pioneering Malaysian Students Technology Conference or MSTC for short, this year we are hoping to direct it towards one of the most transformative and disruptive events happening in our world today. The fourth industrial revolution as it has come to be known as is dramatically changing the way the world works but so few people are aware of it and much less able to stay ahead of the curve and remain competitive in a diverse and ever changing environment. With this conference, we hope to bring Malaysian students which represent the future of our country together with experts in the relevant fields such as big data analytics, cyber-physical systems and many more up and coming fields which we feel the future leaders of our country should have a strong grasp of.

Mr. Ganesh Pramod
Coordinator of MSTC 2017
University of Manchester
