The Malaysian Students' Technology Conference Quote Competition

Simply participate this contest by Thursday, the 31st of July 2016!

Write a 30-word (max) quote and include 2 words 'Malaysia' and 'Technology' or 'Teknologi'.

Eligibility:

  1. This competition is open to every Malaysian students currently study in any universities
  2. Only one entry is allowed for each individual, quote can be writen in English or Malay language
  3. To participate, simply send your entry via Facebook Message or email mssm.mstc2016@gmail.com
  4. We will upload the quotes on our Facebook Page for the public to decide!
  5. Get excited to win a RM 500 Harvey Norman gift card

The deadline is on the 31st of July (11.59pm) for entries and online voting for the quotes will continue till the of 26th of August (11.59pm).

Last update: July 8, 2016
